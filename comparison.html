    Is this a reliable comparison?

    The information we gathered about Disc Makers is from their website, Yelp page, Facebook page, and their terms and conditions page. We try to be fair with our comparison and give the right information.

    What does Unified Manufacturing and Disc Makers have in common?

    Unified Manufacturing and Disc Makers are both leading media replicators in the USA. They both offer a variety of services for musicians, artists, and businesses for releasing an album, film, or product. Both offer the following services: CD replication, CD duplication, DVD duplication & DVD replication, vinyl record pressing, USB replication, merch, and promotional items.

    What are the major reasons why Unified Manufacturing is the better choice than Disc Makers?

    Good customer service and good location. One of the main reasons clients come to us is our location. We are located in LA, right in the heart of the entertainment industry. Not only will this make everything faster, but you can also visit our office anytime to see samples of our previous projects, submit your masters, check on your order, and just hang out with our crew. Although anything can be done remotely, being physically in the office of your manufacturer is definitely a big plus.

    What is the Unified Manufacturing Yelp review score compared to Disc Makers?

    Yelp is still one of the top sites to visit if you’re looking for real reviews by real customers. Disc Makers has an average Yelp rating of 2.5/5 stars from 21 reviewers. Meantime, Unified Manufacturing has an average Yelp rating of 4.0/5 from 38 reviewers.

    What is the Unified Manufacturing Facebook review score compared to Disc Makers?

    Another good and honest indicator of a company’s performance is through Facebook reviews because they are made by real people. Unified Manufacturing’s Facebook page has an average review rating of 4 out of 5 stars from 33 reviewers. On the other hand, Disc Makers Facebook page has disabled their reviews.

    Why is a local business a better choice than a corporate-owned one?

    Disc Makers Ownership: AVL Digital Group -- the parent company of CD Baby, Disc Makers, and other self-publishing platforms — has been sold to Stephens Capital Partners, a private equity group based in Little Rock, Arkansas, Billboard.biz has learned.

    If you choose Unified Manufacturing to make your CD/DVD/vinyl and merch, you are supporting a local business. Local businessesare more accountable to their local communities and donate more money to non-profits. Supporting local businesses is good for the environment because they often have a smaller carbon footprint than larger companies. Disc Makers is a corporation owned by a private equity group.

    Why is a good location important in choosing a CD/DVD/Vinyl records manufacturing company?

    Choosing a company near you or your event will not only guarantee faster shipping, but it’s also good for the environment. There is also more accountability since you can visit the shop anytime to ask for updates, returns, and reorders.

We have proven ourselves to be the go-to place when it comes to creative music box sets, creative DVD box sets, unique vinyl sleeves, and more. Just tell us what you need and we will design and make them from the ground up. No design is too complicated.

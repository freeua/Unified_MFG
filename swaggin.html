    Why partner with SWAGGIN’ for your Merch

    Swaggin’ is owned by Unified Manufacturing, a CD/DVD/vinyl replication company that’s been making albums for big labels as well as indie labels since 2008. This means that if you’re a musician, you get the perks from having your merch done under the same roof.

    We can make CD+vinyl+merch bundles.

    Lorem ipsun Our CEO has worked for major record labels and knows the ins and outs of the business.

    How it works

    Connect your store to Swaggin'

    Set up an online store on your website and upload as many designs as you want for your t-shirts, band buttons, and other merch items.

    When customers order from your website, this will automatically be placed on our system.

    Connect Store

    Swaggin' will create and ship your merch.

    Once a customer places an order from your store, we will be instantly notified.

    We will then manufacture their orders on-demand.

    This means that we will print them on a per-order basis. You will get a tracking number.

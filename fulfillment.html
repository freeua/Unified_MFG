    How it works

    We know you guys are giggin’ and hustlin’ hard so allow us to do the dirty work when it comes to your merch! We will take care of everything — from storing, assembling, packing, to shipping your items so you won’t have to stress & sweat with every order.

    Send us your items
    (Or we make them for you)

    We stock your items in our warehouse

    We ship your items to your fans

    You should partner with us if…

    You love selling stuff but you hate order fulfillment.

    Handling the nitty gritty day-to-day task of order fulfillment can burn you out if it’s not something you want to do. Reserve your energy for something more important.

    You don’t have the space for your items.

    As you make more and more items to sell, you need bigger space to store them. Do you want to store them in your room or basement?

    You want a team who will make your customers feel special.
    We will treat each of your customers as if they’re worth a million bucks even if they only buy one item. This is a guarantee.
